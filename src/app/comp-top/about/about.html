Release Plan

This is the release plan: closed alpha -> closed beta -> public beta -> official release

Private alpha phase

The service is only avalible for friends and family. Streams and signals from this period will not be carried over to beta phase.

Closed beta phase 1

Gradually invite more and more people that has signed up for a beta invite (first come first served). The earlier you are starting a stream, the more statistics you have and the more attractive you stream can become when the availability to subscribe are released (public beta phase).

At the end of this phase, a trading competition with bitcoin rewards will be hosted (all streams on the marketplace are competing).

Closed beta phase 2

Subscriptions become available to anyone.

Official release

Official release. Anyone can sign up and subscribe.
