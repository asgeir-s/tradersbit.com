Release Plan

Release plan: closed alpha -> beta -> official release

Private alpha phase

The service is only available for friends and family. Streams and signals from this period will not be carried over to beta phase.

Beta phase 1

Gradually invite more and more people that has signed up for a beta invite (first come first served). The earlier you are starting a stream, the more statistics you have and the more attractive you stream might become when the availability to subscribe are released (at the end of this phase).

At the end of this phase, a trading competition with Bitcoin rewards will be hosted (all streams on the marketplace are competing) and subscriptions become available to anyone.

Official release

Anyone can both sign up as a publisher and subscriber.
