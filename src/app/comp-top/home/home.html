How it works

TradersBit is a marketplace for streams of Bitcoin trading signals.

Publishers create streams, send trading signals to streams, and sell subscriptions to streams. Statistics for each stream's performance is computed and displayed in the marketplace. The statistics include trading fees (unless explicitly stated otherwise).

Subscribers can browse and sort streams based on average monthly profit, average trade, and other indicators. When subscribing to a stream, the subscriber can get signals sent by email, or the signaled trade can be executed automatically on the subscriber's behalf (coming soon).

The publisher decides the subscription fee (monthly fee), and when a subscription is sold, the publisher immediately receives 70% of the subscription fee.

Upon the reception of a trading signal, we simulate the trade (including trading fees). This way, there is now a way for the publishers to manipulate statistics.

There is no social interaction in this marketplace because the statistics and trading signals speak for themselves. This way, subscribers will not be easily scammed. A subscriber should not know or care whether signals are sent manually by a teenager or an MBA graduate or automatically generated by a trading boot (algorithm trading).

Signals can be published through our website, mobile app, or API.

Our API makes it possible to write trading systems in any programming language and easily integrate it with existing trading systems (bots).

TradersBit will also host trading competitions. For instance, most profitable streams last 30 days, etc. All rewards are in Bitcoins.
