TradersBit is a marketplace for streams of Bitcoin trading signals.

You can make money by subscribing to statistically analyzed streams of trading signals,
or you can publish your own trading signals and sell subscriptions to it.

3 Steps to Subscribe
 1

Find a stream

Browse and sort streams by checking statistics and previous trades.

2

Fill in Subscription Informatin

Click the subscribe button (bottom right corner). Fill in your email address for receiving trading signals, and if you want to use the auto trader, fill in the API keys (coming soon).

3

Pay

Pay with Bitcoin. After payment, you will receive trading signals by email, and/or have the trading signals automatically traded for you.

3 Steps to Publish
 1

Sign Up as a Publisher

Go to "Publish" and click "Sign Up".

 2

Create a Stream

After you have signed in, click the new stream button in the bottom right corner. Fill in a stream name and your Bitcoin address for payouts to you (for subscriptions and contest winnings). Then select initial subscription price.

3

Submit Signals

Click SHORT, CLOSE, or LONG to send signals. Or generate an API key for sending signals through our API.

How it works

TradersBit is a marketplace for streams of Bitcoin trading signals.

Publishers create streams, send trading signals to streams, and sell subscriptions to streams. Statistics for each stream's performance is computed and displayed in the marketplace. The statistics include trading fees (unless explicitly stated otherwise).

Subscribers can browse and sort streams based on average monthly profit, average trade, and other indicators. When subscribing to a stream, the subscriber can get signals sent by email, or the signaled trade can be executed automatically on the subscriber's behalf (coming soon).
