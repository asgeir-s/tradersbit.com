How it works

This is a marketplace for streams of Bitcoin trading signals.

Publishers can create streams, send trading signals to streams and sell subscriptions to streams. Statistics for each streams performance is computed and displayed in the marketplace. The statistics include trading fees (unless explicitly stated otherwise). All closed trades are also displayed.

Subscribers can browse and sort streams based on average monthly profit, average trade profit and other indicators. When subscribing to a stream, the subscriber can get signals sent to his email or automatically executed on his behalf with our autotrader-feature.
