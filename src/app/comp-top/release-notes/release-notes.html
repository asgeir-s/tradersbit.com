Release Notes

BETA 0.3.1 (24 May 2016)
  • Edited some info on the landing page.
  • Turned filters on by default in the streams view.
  • Fixed a bug for the "Highest Net Profit" leaderboard.
BETA 0.3.0 (13 May 2016)
  • Publishers can now connect their Bitfinex account to a stream. The stream will automatically mirror their active position from Bitfinex. (Experimental feature).
BETA 0.2.2 (01 May 2016)
  • Some interface tweaks.
BETA 0.2.1 (29 Apr 2016)
  • Added some filters to the streams page.
BETA 0.2.0 (18 Apr 2016)
  • Autotrader is enabled (read about it in the FAQ).
  • Almost a complete redesign and rewrite of the backend powering TradersBit. This should make TradersBit faster more reliable and speed up development of new features.
BETA 0.1.11 (11 Mar 2016)
  • Added roadmap.
  • Added Twitter
BETA 0.1.10 (03 Mar 2016)
  • The competition leaderboard now reflect the competition rule of minimum 10 closed trades.
  • Five days before a subscription is expiring; the subscriber will get an email with the option to buy a subscription for 30 more days.
BETA 0.1.9 (14 Feb 2016)
  • Publishers can now change the subscription price for theire streams.
BETA 0.1.8 (11 Feb 2016)
  • Publishing is fixed for Firefox.
  • Smoother browsing experience on mobile.
BETA 0.1.7 (10 Feb 2016)
  • Subscribers get a confirmation email. The mail includes the stream's current position.
  • Publishers get a notification email when they sell subscriptions.
  • Some small UI changes for mobile and some fixed typos.
BETA 0.1.6 (08 Feb 2016)
  • Added fees for both opening and closing positions in "unrealized PL" in the "publisher-dash" and notifications. This is only a interface change. Thus, it does not affect any statistics.
  • Fixed profit charts.
  • Enhanced signup, sign in and password rest flow.
  • Change date format to a universally understandable format.
  • Top stream at landing page is now the streams with highest Net Profit.
  • Fixed calculation of indicator 'Net Profit Excluding fees'.
